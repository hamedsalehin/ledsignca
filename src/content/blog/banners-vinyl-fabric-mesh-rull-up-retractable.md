## **most popular banner types:**

###### 1\. Rull-Up & Retractable Banners: are an attractive, double-sided solution for tradeshow events and indoor retail signage. Using cables and rods, hanging banners can be displayed above crowds for maximum visibility and promotional impact. While installation for ceiling displays may be a little more involved.

###### 2\. Mesh Banners: stands can be placed both indoors and outdoors and are known for The weave of a mesh banner is more permeable, allowing wind to pass through without lifting the banner up like a sailor wing. The major benefit of such a construction is that it allows the banner to brave harsh winds and inclement weather outside, while still serving the primary function of branding or advertising.

###### 3\. Fabric Banners, usually consisting of polyester or satin, provide stunning full-colored graphics that will captivate and awe. Machine-washable and crease-resistant (unlike vinyl banners), these displays are the perfect solution for any business that needs a high-quality banner with little or no setup time.

###### 4\. Vinyl Banners: are a form of outdoor [advertising](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Advertising "Advertising"). Most banners are now digitally printed on [large format](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wide-format_printer "Wide-format printer") [inkjet](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Inkjet "Inkjet") printers which are capable of printing a full-color outdoor [billboard](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Billboard_\(advertising\) "Billboard (advertising)") on a single piece of material. Vinyl banners are an old standby and for good reason. It’s hard to argue with the durability of their material; they can be used both indoors and outdoors, and they are tear-resistant.

Advertising banner design principles for greater efficiency

Once you have found the right tool for banner design, you need to start designing it based on the principles of banner design. Banners can be customized to suit the designer's tastes, but it is important to pay attention to certain points to make the ads more effective and to get the maximum return. Otherwise, a banner may be designed that has no positive effect and only takes up space on the web page.

In the following, we describe a number of important principles for designing a banner ad with maximum efficiency that are observed in the images above.

Call to action

Call to Action (CTA) is an element that encourages potential customers to learn more about a business service or product.

Refers to the brand

Your brand logo should be in the ad, otherwise people will not understand what business they are dealing with the ad. The logo can be anywhere in the ad and does not need to take up much space or grab people's attention and should only be included in the banner so that it can be distinguished from the other visual elements of the ad.

Use high quality images

Using quality images in banners will make your ads look professional.

Advertising images should be interesting and convey its message directly to the visitor. Paying attention to the rules of color combination and the harmony and psychology of colors can help to create quality and effective advertising.
