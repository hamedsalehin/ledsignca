# Indoor Full Color LED Display with SMD technology provides a seamless, high-resolution, high-contrast display solution for interior areas with short viewing distances. Its compact and lightweight design guarantees easy, quick installation. With an aspect ratio of 16:9, contrast ratio of over 5,000:1, a light output of over 800nits, and higher color processing, the 1.5mm LED delivers excellent image and video quality at an optimal viewing distance of >1.5m.
